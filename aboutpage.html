About

Kindergarten, primary and secondary education 11-50 employees. 10 on linkedin

Halley Internation School is an award-winning educational environment that allows students not only to learn, but connect with nature and engage with their community. creating the oppurtunity to truly thrive. Our mission in HIS communtiy where children learn and shine through authentic experiences in nature to become engaged global citizens. Nature based education supports children's developement in social and emotional intelligence, problem solving and environmental understanding.

The natural environment offers children and young people the chance to learn about themselves and the world around them and builds resilience they can use throughout life. At HIS we take inquiry-based learnig approach, fostering our students' abilities to explore the real world around them and learn through doing. All our teachers share our philosophy and passion for nature-based education and encourage students to take calculated risks, make mistakes, presist, think laterally, assess situations from different perspectives and work cooperatively. The early years program (3-5yr olds) is project-based outdoors, while primary students have regular adventure days and secondary students embark on feild studies.
